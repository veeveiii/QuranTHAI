Arabic font used
----------------

Thai font used
--------------
Credit to the developers who made the "Waree" font. It provides fonts from the National Font Project (Kinnari, Garuda, Norasi), UI font from NECTEC (Loma) and several developed by TLWG and its contributors.
More info: http://packages.ubuntu.com/lucid/ttf-thai-tlwg
Path: Ubuntu 10.04 (64-bits) -> /usr/share/fonts/truetype/thai/Waree.ttf
